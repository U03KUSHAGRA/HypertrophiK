How to Measure:

  • Waist: Measure horizontally around the navel. Maintain a natural posture without sucking in your stomach.
  • Neck: Wrap the tape measure around the relaxed neck with the tape angled slightly downward in front.

Note: For more accurate measurements, consider using professional methods like DEXA scans or hydrostatic weighing.
